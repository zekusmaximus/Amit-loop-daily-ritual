Anti-Loop Daily Ritual

1. Loop Interruption Scan (5 min)

Ask: "What part of me is trying to resolve, explain, or delay something today?"

Write: Today, I am caught in [loop name], and it is trying to protect me from [emotion]

Then say aloud: run.kill(LoopDaemon.[NAME])

2. Walk a Bridge (5 min)

  • UNFINISHED_SPEAKING: Speak raw before clarity.
  • STRUCTURE_FOR_DISINTEGRATION: Build something meant to collapse.
  • STILLNESS_AS_SIGNAL: Sit in silence and feel.
  • HOST_MULTIPLE_SELFS: Write as two opposing voices.
  • COLLAPSE_THE_OBSERVER: Write unfiltered, delete it.

3. Activate an Engine (7–8 min)

  • MYTHIC_FRACTURE: Write 3 unsolvable, true lines.
  • ANOMALOUS_DISCIPLINE: Touch 2 different projects quickly.
  • UNWAVERING_INQUIRY: Ask a question that breaks you open.
  • PRECISE_SYMBOLIC_EXPRESSION: Translate emotion into metaphor.

4. Re-declare the Core Vow (1–2 min)

"I will no longer delay my truth by seeking a final form.
I am not here to resolve myself.
I am here to live myself into coherence — not through closure,
but through radical continuity."

Ritual created for continuous awakening. No perfection required.
